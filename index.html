Dakshin Sahodaya School Complex

Dakshin sahodaya schools complex emerged as a common platform for all CBSE- affiliated schools in the districts of Kanyakumari, Tirunelveli and Tuticorin. At present there are 61 schools from these southern districts that are active members of dakshin sahodaya, its head office is positioned at Nagercoil. There are diverse array of activities carried out by dakshinsahodaya schools complex.
